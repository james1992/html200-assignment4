Ready for an Adventure?

How fast can you find the treasure that is hidden somewhere in the grid below? When you press the button below the game will start. The treasure will be randomly placed somewhere in the 10 x 10 grid and you will start in the lower left at 0,0. The game will prompt you to first provide your name and then will ask for a direction of travel (north, south, east or west). North and South will move you up and down the Y axis respectively while east and west will move you right and left along the same row. You can only move to one adacent square at a time and your move must be within the grid, if your move would put you out of the grid the game will prompt you to provide a new direction.

You will receive hints along the way that will tell you if you are heading in the right direction (these will appear in the console). At the conclusion of the game, after you find the treasure the grid below will highlight the path you took during the game. Each cell that you traveled to will be filled in green and the treasure location will be gold.

Click below to start your adventure when you are ready!
